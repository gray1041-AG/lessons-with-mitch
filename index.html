Common Questions

How much do guitar lessons cost in Kenosha?

Lessons with Mitch are $25 for a 30-minute session or $50 for a 60-minute session. There are no contracts or commitments — pay per lesson and cancel anytime. Your first lesson is free so you can see if it's a good fit.

What ages do you teach?

All ages — there's no minimum age and no prerequisites. Mitch teaches kids, teens, and adults at every experience level, from complete beginners to advanced players looking to level up.

Do you teach bass and ukulele too, or just guitar?

Mitch teaches guitar, bass, and ukulele. Lessons cover chords, music theory, songs you love, songwriting, and performing — adapted to whichever instrument you play.

Where are lessons held?

In-person lessons are held at The Music Center in Kenosha, WI. Online lessons are also available if you prefer to learn from home. Either format works — pick what's most convenient.

Do I need my own instrument?

Having your own instrument to practice on at home is ideal, but it's not required to get started. Reach out and Mitch can help point you in the right direction if you need to rent or buy one.

Can I book Mitch for a live show or private event?

Yes — Mitch performs solo acoustic sets and full band shows for bars, venues, festivals, weddings, and private events. Visit the bookings page to send a request.
