Lessons With Mitch

Mitch is a musician, songwriter, and instructor who has been playing and performing music for most of his life. He helps people of all ages reach their musical goals with an individualized approach for every student.

Whether you're picking up a guitar for the first time or you've been playing for years and want to level up — lessons are built around you.

Lessons are designed to go at your own pace and adapt as your skills and interests change.

Beyond the Lesson

Music is meant to be shared. As students progress, Mitch encourages real-world playing — open mics, jam sessions, and playing with others any chance you get.

Group classes are planned to help students build confidence playing together outside the practice room.

"The focus is not perfection. It's progress, expression, and enjoying the process."
